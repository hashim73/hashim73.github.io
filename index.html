Recipe #1 Sunny Side eggs with toast

Description: A simple and easy breakfast

  • Time: about 15 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person..

ingredients

2 tbsp butter

3 slices of white bread

1 egg

1/2 tsp salt

1/2 teaspoon blackpepper

Step 1:

Preheat your pan to medium heat.

Step 2:

Butter each side of your bread and let it heat up on the oven until one side is light gold, then flip the bread and let the other side change colour.

Step 3:

Crack your egg open on the pan and let it cook. After a minute flip the egg and let the other side cook. Then once each side has cooked take it of the pan and put your bread and eggs on the plate. Put the salt and pepper on the egg and serve.

Recipe #2: Mozzarella Sticks

These are very good and taste delicious. Pizza lovers would love these!

  • Time: About 15 minutes
  • Serves: About 6-8

ingredients

1⁄4 cup flour

1 cup Italian style breadcrumbs

1 tablespoon milk

1 lb mozzarella cheese, cut into 3/4 inch x 3/4 inch strips (or you can use string cheese and cut each in half)

1 cup vegetable oil

1 cup jarred pizza sauce or 1 cup marinara sauce

Step 1:

Whisk eggs and milk together.

Step 2:

Coat each piece of cheese with flour, then dip in egg and then roll in bread crumbs.

Step 3:

Dip in egg and crumbs again.

Step 4:

Freeze before frying

Step 5:

Heat oil in skillet and cook sticks for about a minute on each side, or until golden and before cheese starts to leak.

Step 6:

Drain on paper towels and serve with dipping sauce

Recipe #3: Chocolate Chip cookies!

These cooks are easy to make and delicious they are soft on the inside and crispy on the outside.

  • Time: About an hour
  • Servings: 24

Ingredients

1 cup butter, softened

1 cup white sugar

1 cup packed brown sugar

2 eggs

2 teaspoons vanilla extract

1 teaspoon baking soda

2 teaspoons hot water.)

½ teaspoon salt

3 cups all-purpose flour

2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

1 cup chopped walnuts

Step 1:

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

Step 2:

Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.

Step 3:

Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.
